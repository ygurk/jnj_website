Client testimonial — the strongest trust signal. Always use a real name + business; add a photo where you can.

```jsx
<Testimonial rating={5}
  quote="They made the whole thing painless and our bookings doubled."
  name="Maria Alvarez" business="Alvarez Family Dental" photo="/maria.jpg" />

<Testimonial featured quote="..." name="..." business="..." />
```

Props: `quote`, `name`, `business`, `photo`, `rating` (0–5), `featured` (larger hero variant). Composes `Avatar`.
